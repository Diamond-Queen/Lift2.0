## 5. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard safeguards including:
- **Encryption**: HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit (encrypted connections)
- **At-Rest Encryption**: AES-256 encryption for data stored in databases
- **Password Security**: Argon2 password hashing with salt
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication (MFA) available on accounts
- **Network Security**: Firewall protection, rate limiting, DDoS prevention
- **Access Control**: Role-based access controls and principle of least privilege
- **Audit Logging**: All data access is logged and monitored for suspicious activity
- **Regular Security**: Periodic security audits and penetration testing
- **Secure Cookies**: HTTP-only, secure, SameSite cookie settings
- **Session Management**: Automatic session timeout after 24 hours of inactivity

**Security Limitations**:
- Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure
- We recommend using strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication for additional protection
- Be cautious with phishing emails and suspicious links
- Report security vulnerabilities to williams.lift101@gmail.com

**Breach Notification**:
- If we discover a security breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours
- Notifications will be sent via email and in-app notification
- We will cooperate with law enforcement investigations as required

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Right to Correction**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Right to Deletion**: Request permanent removal of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Stop receiving marketing communications

### 7.2 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)
If you are in the European Union, you have:

- Right to rectification of inaccurate data
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

### 7.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of personal data (with exceptions)
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of selling or sharing personal information
- **Right to Correct**: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights
- **Right to Limit**: Limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information

### 7.4 How to Exercise Your Rights
To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:
- **Email**: williams.lift101@gmail.com
- **Response Time**: Within 30 days (or 45 days for GDPR requests)
- **Required Information**: We may ask you to verify your identity before processing requests
- **No Charge**: Most requests are free; we may charge for excessive or frivolous requests

### 7.5 Right to Appeal
If we deny your request, you have the right to appeal. Contact us with your appeal.

## 8. COOKIES & TRACKING

### 8.1 Types of Cookies We Use
We use cookies for:
- **Authentication**: To keep you logged in across sessions
- **Session Management**: To track your active session
- **Security**: To prevent unauthorized access and detect fraud
- **Fraud Prevention**: To identify and block suspicious activity
- **Analytics**: To understand how you use Lift (anonymized)

### 8.2 Cookie Policy
- We do NOT use cookies for third-party advertising or marketing
- Cookies are set with `Secure` and `HttpOnly` flags for protection
- Cookies are set with `SameSite=Strict` to prevent CSRF attacks
- Session cookies expire after 24 hours of inactivity
- Persistent cookies expire after 1 year

### 8.3 Your Cookie Control
- You can disable cookies in your browser settings
- Disabling cookies may limit functionality (e.g., you may need to re-login)
- We do not use tracking pixels or third-party trackers
- You can clear your cookies anytime in your browser settings

### 8.4 Third-Party Trackers
We do NOT use:
- Google Analytics or similar tracking services
- Facebook Pixel or social media trackers
- Third-party advertising networks
- Session recorders or heatmap tools that violate privacy
